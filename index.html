Welcome to Me as an AI chatbot

Thanks for stopping by. I built this AI chatbot while completing some Agentic AI training, partly as a way to explore what’s now possible with modern AI—and partly to offer a simple, interactive way for prospective employers and clients to get to know me. This bot can answer questions about my background, experience, and the kind of work I do, as well as demonstrate how I approach problem-solving, communication, and technology. Think of it as a smarter, more engaging version of a CV: active, conversational, and available whenever you are. Feel free to ask anything, try things out, and explore. I hope this gives you a useful glimpse into my skills and how I think about bringing AI into real-world operations and solutions. If you’d like to talk further, I’m always happy to connect.
